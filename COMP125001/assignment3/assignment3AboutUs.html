Agent Profile

 

Peter Golab launched his real estate career after several years in corporate aviation. His clients included some of the biggest real estate developers in the Toronto and GTA areas. Initially Peter used his contacts from aviation and his network to focus on development acquisitions, and successfully managed to broker several large parcels for subdivision. Other developments later included green field and brown field developments, many infill projects, and multiple single build lot for custom home builders. Peter’s experiences with his several fantastic builders, developers, as well as some real estate professionals, helped shape his core values: service, respect, trust, excellence results.
These values are front center in the representation he gives to his clients to help them accomplish their real estate goals.
